About

Bubby's World became the 51st state in late 2024. In early 2025, actual full development and colonization of Bubby's World started. Then along came the first governor of the state, ProOfficial, in 2030. In the year 2090, ProOfficial became the permanent president of the U.S. while Bubby became the governor of the state.



The origin of Bubby's World

Bubby's World started out as a boring old superflat in Minecraft PE in the year 2022. Then, one week after creation, Bubby the Builder came along and built the first neighborhood in the state. He was originally going to sell them for $10m, but instead lowered it to $7.1m. While the neighborhood was getting finished, ProOfficial built the first power plant to power the tiny city. Further developement of stores, restaraunts, other neighnorhoods, and more power plants started. Today, Bubby's World is home to over 500+ homes, 50+ stores & restaraunts, 6 power plants, and several huge towers & famous landmarks.
